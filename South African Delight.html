ITINERARY


DAY 1 ARRIVE CAPE TOWN

Welcome to South Africa! On arrival in Cape Town you will be met at the airport and privately transferred to your hotel. Enjoy the rest of the day at leisure. Cape Town is one of the most popular long haul destinations in the world. Due to its unique topography, it is also one of the easiest places in which to orientate oneself as long as you remember that with Table Mountain behind you and Robben Island before you; you are facing north, looking across Table Bay and up the west coast of Africa. Overnight at Cape Town.

DAY 2 CAPE TOWN - HALF DAY CITY TOUR INCLUDING TABLE MOUNTAIN (B/-/-)

Today you will enjoy a half day city tour including Table Mountain. (Weather permitting). Visit South African Museum & the company Gardens, Parliament buildings, City Hall & castle of good Hope, Malay Quarter / Bo-Kaap, Green Market Square, Signal Hill, Camps Bay & Clifton. This afternoon, you are at leisure, we recommend spending some time at the V&A Waterfront which has a wide variety of shops, entertainment and restaurants on offer. Overnight at Cape Town.

DAY 3 CAPE TOWN FULL DAY GUIDED TOUR OF THE BEAUTIFUL CAPE PENINSULA (B/-/-)

Enjoy full day guided tour of the beautiful Cape Peninsula, highlights of the tour includes Clifton & Camps Bay, Twelve Apostles, Llandudno, Hout Bay (optional boat trip to Seal Isalnd), Chapmans Peak Drive (if open), Noordhoek, Ostrich viewing (drive by), Cape of Good Hope Nature Reserve, Cape Point, Cape of Good Hope, Boulders Beach Penguin Colony, Simons Town, Muizenberg, Constantia, Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens. Overnight at Cape Town.

DAY 4 CAPE TOWN – KNYSNA (B/-/-)

Today you will travel to the Garden Route, your destination is Knysna and you will enjoy a relaxing sunset cruise on the Knysna Lagoon. Overnight in Knysna.

DAY 5 KNYSNA (B/-/-)

Today you will be taken on a full day visit to Oudtshoorn where you will enjoy guided tours of the Cango Caves, Cango Wildlife Ranch and an ostrich farm. Return to your Knysna hotel in the late afternoon. Overnight at Knysna.

DAY 6 CAPE TOWN – KNYSNA (B/-/-)

Today you will travel into the Eastern Cape to your Big Five private game reserve for a safari experience. Enjoy your first afternoon game drive, followed by dinner at the lodge. Kwantu Private Game Reserve is situated a mere 85km from Port Elizabeth on the N2 national highway towards Grahamstown. Overnight in Kwantu Private Game Reserve. (Lunch is time permitting on arrival at the lodge)

DAY 7 KNYSNA – KWANTU PRIVATE GAME RESERVE (B/L/D)

Today you will travel to the Garden Route, your destination is Knysna and you will enjoy a relaxing sunset cruise on the Knysna Lagoon. Overnight in Knysna.

DAY 8 DEPART KWANTU PRIVATE GAME RESERVE - PORT ELIZABETH (B/-/-)

Today after your final morning game drive (time dependent) you will be transferred to Port Elizabeth Airport for your departure flight.
