Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are your candles really handmade?

    Yes! Every candle at The Scented Corner is 100% handmade with care. From pouring to packaging, each step is done by hand to ensure quality and uniqueness.

  • Do you use natural ingredients?

    Absolutely. We use natural fragrances and high-quality wax — free from synthetic perfumes, cosmetic additives, or harmful chemicals. Our candles are safe, clean-burning, and crafted with your well-being in mind.

  • What types of candles do you make?

    We create a variety of shaped and styled candles, ranging from minimalist to artistic designs. All our candles are made to be both decorative and functional.

  • Are your candles scented?

    Yes, most of our candles are scented using natural fragrance oils. However, we also offer unscented options — perfect for those who prefer a more subtle experience.

  • Do you accept custom or bulk orders?

    We’re happy to take custom and bulk orders, especially for events, gifts, or special occasions. Just reach out to us via our contact form or social media to discuss details.

  • How long do your candles burn?

    Burn time varies depending on the size and shape of the candle, but on average, our candles burn for 15 to 40 hours. We recommend trimming the wick and following our care tips for the best results.

  • Where do you ship?

    We currently ship nationwide and are working on expanding to international locations. For shipping details, check our Shipping Policy page or contact us directly.

  • How can I care for my candle?

    To get the most from your candle:

    • Trim the wick to ¼ inch before each use.
    • Let the candle burn until the wax melts evenly across the top.
    • Avoid burning for more than 4 hours at a time.
    • Keep away from drafts, children, and pets.
